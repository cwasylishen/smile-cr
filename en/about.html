About Us

Meet our team and the story behind Smile Dental Clinic in Santa Ana, Costa Rica.

Our Story

A Clinic Built Around Your Smile

Smile Dental Clinic started with a simple idea: going to the dentist doesn't have to be a bad experience. Located in Santa Ana, we take care of you with patience, explain everything clearly, and treat you like a person — not a number.

From day one, the clinic has grown because patients come back and refer their friends and family. That only happens when people feel genuinely well taken care of.

Our Mission

For every patient to leave with a better smile and actually want to come back — thanks to genuine care and treatments that really work.

Our Vision

When anyone in Santa Ana or the surrounding area thinks "dentist," we want Smile to be the first name that comes to mind. Simple as that.

Our Values

Honesty, empathy, and dedication. If you don't need something, we won't recommend it. That's how straightforward we work.

Trusted Professionals

Our Team

The people behind your smile.

General Dentist · Founder

Dr. Jessika Barquero, DDS

A graduate of the University of Costa Rica (UCR) and registered with Costa Rica's Dental Association (Code 309206). Dr. Barquero handles general and cosmetic dentistry — smile design, veneers, implants, and everything in between. Dr. Jessika is fully bilingual and speaks fluent English, so you'll always feel comfortable discussing your treatment.

"I chose the career my heart told me to follow. I love what I do: giving people their smiles back."

Orthodontics Specialist
