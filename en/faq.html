Frequently Asked Questions

Services, hours, payment plans, and more. The questions we hear most, answered.

We do it all: Smile Design, Dental Veneers in porcelain and ceramic, Orthodontics with brackets, Dental Implants in titanium, professional Teeth Whitening, and General Dentistry (cleanings, crowns, bridges, extractions, and emergencies). You'll be cared for by Dr. Jessika Barquero, DDS, and Dr. Veronica Fernandez, our orthodontics specialist.

You can start your braces treatment without a large upfront payment. We have accessible payment plans so it doesn't become a financial burden. Send us a message on WhatsApp and we'll walk you through the options for your case.

Yes, we handle dental emergencies 24/7. If you have severe pain, a broken tooth, a loose implant, or any urgent situation, message us right now on WhatsApp at +506 8811-7451 and we'll take care of you as soon as possible.

We're in Santa Ana, San Jose, Costa Rica. Not sure how to get here? Message us on WhatsApp and we'll be happy to give you directions.

All appointments are booked through WhatsApp at +506 8811-7451. Send us a message with what you need and we'll find a day and time that works. You can tap the WhatsApp button on this page to reach us directly.

We work with private payments, but we offer flexible payment plans to make treatments more accessible. If you'd like to know about financing options or have questions about a specific plan, message us on WhatsApp and we'll sort it out together.

Dr. Jessika will do a complete check-up: a visual exam, an evaluation of your gums and teeth, and X-rays if needed. Then we'll sit down with you to talk through what you need, your goals, and put together a plan with clear options and costs. The atmosphere here is calm. No pressure, no rush.

The porcelain and ceramic veneers we use last between 10 and 20 years with proper care. This depends on your daily hygiene, not biting hard objects, not grinding your teeth, and keeping up with your regular check-ups. Done right, they hold up for a long time.

Professional whitening lasts between 1 and 3 years, depending on how much coffee, tea, or tobacco you consume and your daily hygiene. Avoiding staining foods and drinks in the first few days after treatment helps the results last longer. A yearly touch-up keeps things looking fresh, or whenever Dr. Jessika recommends it.

Orthodontic treatment is handled by Dr. Veronica Fernandez, our in-house specialist. We offer traditional metal brackets and other options depending on your case. All orthodontic treatments are available under the No Down Payment Program, with accessible monthly plans so you can get started without a heavy upfront cost.

We're open Monday through Saturday with flexible hours to fit your schedule. For emergencies, we're available 24 hours a day, every day. To book or check availability, message us on WhatsApp at +506 8811-7451.

Dr. Jessika Barquero is a Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) from the University of Costa Rica (UCR), registered with Costa Rica's Dental Association under code 309206. She's the director of Smile Dental Clinic, with years of experience in general and cosmetic dentistry. She's fully bilingual, takes the time to explain everything clearly, and will never recommend something you don't actually need.
