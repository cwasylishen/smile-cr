Cosmetic Dentistry

Dental Veneers

Veneers are ultra-thin porcelain or ceramic shells that bond directly to your teeth to correct imperfections. They're durable, and the results are visible right away.

They work well for stained, chipped, worn, gapped, or uneven teeth. Porcelain catches the light similarly to natural enamel, so the result is hard to distinguish from your real teeth.

With proper care, veneers last between 10 and 20 years. We use top-quality materials so your results stay looking great for a long time.

Implantology

Dental Implants

Implants are the best way to replace missing teeth. A titanium root is placed in the jawbone and topped with a crown that looks exactly like a natural tooth.

Unlike bridges or dentures, implants don't rely on neighboring teeth and are far more comfortable. With good care, they can last a lifetime.

We perform a thorough evaluation with X-rays to determine if implants are the right option for your case, and walk you through every step before we begin.

High-Quality Titanium Permanent Results Looks Like a Natural Tooth

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening is one of our most popular treatments. Results show up fast. We use clinical-grade products that lighten your teeth several shades in just a few sessions.

It's not the same as over-the-counter products. In-office treatment uses professional-strength formulas with gum protection, so results last longer and the difference is real.

We also provide take-home kits to help you maintain your whiteness over time. Before we start, we check that your teeth and gums are healthy and ready.
