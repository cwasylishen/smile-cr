Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Jessika Barquero, DDS

Dr. Jessika Barquero is a Dental Surgeon who graduated from the University of Costa Rica (UCR), registered with the Costa Rican Dental Surgeons Association under code 309206. She genuinely loves what she does, and patients notice it from the very first visit.

"I chose the career my heart told me to. I love what I do: giving people smiles."

At Smile Dental Clinic, we take time to listen and walk you through every option before starting any treatment. No surprises, no pressure. Just good dentistry done right.

Dr. Jessika is fully fluent in English, so you can ask questions, understand your options, and feel confident about your care.
